    The source

    “Film Locations in San Francisco” — dataset yitu-d5am on DataSF, maintained by the San Francisco Film Commission. One row per logged location per production.

    What's mapped

    The 2,118 rows that carry coordinates, across 295 productions. A handful of un-geocoded entries and off-map outliers are set aside. Decade is taken from each title's earliest release year.

    Honest gaps

    This is a permit-and-publicity logbook, not a complete filmography — older films are sparsely recorded and many shoots never make it in. It shows where the city let itself be filmed, not every frame ever shot here. Last refreshed —.
