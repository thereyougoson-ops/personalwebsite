Method & notes

How this is made

The data

Every tree comes from the Public Works Street Tree List — DPW-maintained street trees, updated daily.

Snapshot refreshed —. Re-run pipeline/build_forest.py to update.

The colors

Each tree is colored by its botanical genus (the first word of its species name). The most common genera get a hand-picked hue; the long tail shares a muted sage.

The timeline

“Watch it grow” shows only the 70,065 trees with a recorded planting date (1955–present) appearing year by year.

Read with care

This is the city's record of street trees, not a tree census — it omits park trees, yard trees, and anything unrecorded.

About 12,303 trees have no species logged; they show as “unidentified.”
